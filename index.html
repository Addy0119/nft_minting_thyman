About US

 

The team at Solgiraffes aims to build a community that cares about the wildlife of this planet, namely the wildlife living in the national parks in Africa that are often underfunded and understaffed which leads to a loss of sustainability of their environment. This habitat loss will ultimately result in the threat of a species. Poaching and insufficient monitoring inside the parks are also factors which also add to the threat. We want to help these national parks and non-profit organizations protect the wildlife that lives there. Our NFT project is made to support these species and will help to raise as much money as we possible can. To ensure that our donations will be well-spent, we will be in contact with the national parks and we will share our road to helping these species survive in the discord. Also to keep the community engaged with our cause we will be flying lucky holders out to one of the local parks to help us with volunteering.
